Welcome to YAIR-WEB!

YAIR-WEB is your Reddit inbox, reloaded. Click the "Authorize with Reddit" button below to allow YAIR-WEB to access your account to send and recieve private messages. YAIR-WEB does not have access to your password, and you can see exactly what YAIR-WEB will have access to before you allow it to connect to your Reddit account.

Please note that you'll need to be logged into the Reddit account you wish to before clicking the Authorize button below. Additionally, if you're accessing YAIR-WEB from an incognito window, YAIR-WEB specific features (folder/archiving) will not save after you close the window, nor will your authorization be stored.
